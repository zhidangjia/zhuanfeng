ABOUT ME - Zhu Anfeng

About 425 wordsAbout 1 min

2023-04-02

  • Over 20 years of experience in IT projects and product management, with full lifecycle capabilities from initial concept development to final delivery and market sales. Worked in various sectors including government and enterprises, telecommunications, and defense.

  • Goal-oriented mindset with rich solution capabilities; strong communication skills, adept at explaining complex business scenarios to project stakeholders, and has served as the leader of provincial-level projects on multiple occasions.

  • Excellent learning and innovation capabilities, as well as business planning skills, having pioneered several business areas from scratch and adapted to rapidly changing and uncertain environments.

  • Good self-motivation, actively refining effective work methods, and has received multiple industry awards.

  • Possesses an open and collaborative attitude, firmly believing that a team of outstanding individuals can achieve greater success.

  • Good physical fitness and stress resistance, with a passion for long-distance running and other sports.

Educational Background

  • Junior and Senior High School: Attended Jingde Middle School in Anhui Province. Achieved the top score in the county for science in the 1999 college entrance examination.

  • University: Studied at Peking University, Department of Computer Science and Technology.

Professional Experience

  • 2006 - 2014: Worked at Jiangsu Post and Telecommunications Planning and Design Institute (now known as China Communications Services Consulting & Design Research Institute Co., Ltd.), focusing on business development in the field of informatization until leaving in early 2014.

  • 2014 - 2018: Joined Telecom Operator System (Jiangsu Telecom), working on the development and operation of mobile internet products.

  • 2018: Allied with a startup founded by a Peking University alumnus (Nanjing Wanggan Zhicha), focusing on the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP).

  • 2020 - Present: Employed by a subsidiary of China Electronics Technology Group Corporation (CETC), contributing to the national defense cause.

Main Honors

  • In 2010, led the "Digital Dafeng Port Information Planning" project, which was awarded the Excellent Engineering Consulting Achievement (First Prize) by Jiangsu Province.

  • In November 2012, as a core member in the development of the "Cloud-based Rui Intelligent Energy Consumption Monitoring and Management Decision Analysis System," received the Nanjing Science and Technology Award (Third Prize).

  • In June 2015, as the core product leader, was honored with the Jiangsu Internet Finance Innovation Enterprise Award.

  • In September 2016, as a principal creator involved in the "Data Center VR Visualization Management Platform," received the Fifth China Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition Military-to-Civilian Award (Third Prize).
