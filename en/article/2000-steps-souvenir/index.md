I have participated in Tencent's step-donation activity for years, and I have finally passed 20 million donated steps. I checked the record specially: the first time I joined this activity was on August 27, 2016.

In a person's life, there are not many things one can remember to do every day and keep doing for more than nine years. The only real difficulty here is walking more than 6,000 steps a day and then remembering to donate them before 10 p.m.

Once again, I felt the power of time in a very concrete way. Even for such a small thing, if you keep doing it regularly, the result can still be meaningful.

Looking back, in most periods of my life, direction, ability, and persistence have indeed felt like an impossible triangle.

From now on, I should remind myself more often how those twenty million steps were actually achieved.
